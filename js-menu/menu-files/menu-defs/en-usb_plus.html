Teachers and Librarians: your class handouts (PDF content, photos, public notices etc) will instantly Appear Right Here after you insert a USB stick/drive into the Internet-in-a-Box.

To publish files, your files MUST first be placed into a folder called 'usb' on your USB stick/drive! Finally: don't hesitate to physically remove your USB stick at any time, after Wi-Fi users have downloaded what they need from you.
